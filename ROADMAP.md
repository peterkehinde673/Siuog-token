# Siuog Token Roadmap

Strategic roadmap for Siuog Token development through 2025 and beyond.

## Vision

Enable direct, frictionless value transfer between creators and supporters within decentralized social networks, starting with the Kehinde Labs ecosystem.

**Status**: In Progress

### Completed
- ✅ ERC-20 token contract with burn mechanism
- ✅ Basic tipping smart contract
- ✅ Escrow vault implementation
- ✅ TypeScript SDK (ethers.js integration)
- ✅ Documentation and guides
- ✅ Security audit (in progress)

### In Progress
- 🔄 Integration with Canopy testnet
- 🔄 Platform testing with early partners
- 🔄 Community setup (Discord, governance)

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Risk**: Smart contract vulnerability
- **Mitigation**: Professional audits, bug bounty program, gradual rollout

### Market Risks
- **Risk**: Crypto regulatory changes
- **Mitigation**: Legal consultation, jurisdiction diversity

### Adoption Risks
- **Risk**: Low platform adoption
- **Mitigation**: Direct partnership development, incentive programs
